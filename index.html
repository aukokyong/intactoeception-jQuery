[In-[tac-toe]-ception]

Be the first to win 3 cells in a row

Click any cell to start

How to Play

  • This is a 2 players game, to be played within 9 cells marked as shown:
    • C1 C2 C3
    C4 C5 C6
    C7 C8 C9
  • Each cell is further divided into 9 sub-cells as shown:
    • 1 2 3
    4 5 6
    7 8 9
  • Each player takes turn to place their marking on any sub-cells that are highlighted in yellow. The sub-cell where a player places his/her marking will determine the sub-cell(s) next player can place next.
  • Within each cell, the first player to have 3 consecutive sub-cells in a row (vertically, horizontally or diagonally), wins that cell.
  • First player to win 3 consecutive cells in a row (vertically, horizontally or diagonally), wins the game.
